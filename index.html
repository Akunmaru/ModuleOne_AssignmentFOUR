About Yambaru, Okinawa

Yanbaru National Park is a national park in Okinawa Prefecture, Japan. Established in 2016, it is located in and around the forested region of Yanbaru at the northern end of Okinawa Island. Yambaru contains four unique hiking trails. All trails start at the Seikigoya hut, a rest spot where food and beverages are available.

Yambaru Trails Contain:

  • Lookout to Mount Hedo
  • Wheelchair access
  • Many natural rock formations
  • Ocean views
  • And MORE!
